Meet Ethical Guidelines and Professional Standards

Know when to refer clients to other support professionals.

Establish the Coaching Agreement

Understand what is required during a coaching session/program, establish clear boundaries, and come to a mutually beneficial agreement with the client.

Establish Trust and Intimacy with the Client

Create a safe, supportive environment that promotes a trusting, respectful client/coach relationship.

Coaching Presence

Be fully present during client sessions and exude a style that is open, flexible, and confident.

Active Listening

Listen fully to what the client does and does not say. Understand the client’s needs and desires, and support his/her self expression.

Powerful (High Mileage) Questioning

Ask questions that evoke informative and in-depth responses from the client.

Direct Communication

Use language that has a direct and positive impact on the client.

Create Awareness

Understand the needs of the client. Help him/her achieve the goals that you set together.

Design Actions

Create opportunities for ongoing learning. Help the client take actions that will effectively lead to accomplishing his/her goals.

Plan and Set Goals

Develop and maintain an effective coaching plan with the client.

Manage Progress and Accountability

Hold the client’s attention on what is important. Instill in the client a sense of responsibility and a desire to take action.

Beginner’s Mind

Approach each session without preconceived notions or judgments.

6-Month Program – Goal Setting

Create both short and long-term goals that fit within the 6-month program format.

Communicate About Health

Apply diet, lifestyle, and primary food concepts to sessions based on the individual needs of the client.
